Recent works:

Beat Defenders

Description:
[Beat Defenders] is a rhythm based tower defense game. An evil ex-roadie turned city counselor, [Lester B. Tonedeaf], is attempting to have Rock performances banned in your small city. Your job as the player is to scare the anti-rock citizens supporting this motion away... How? By practicing your rock as loud as you can of course!

Blending elements of music and rhythm games with that of a tower defense, [Beat Defenders] is a fun game for everyone!

Release Date: TBA

Rating: 7+

The Arena

Description:
The Arena is game where you fight monsters until you have defeated a certain amount of until the hero has died. It includes gaining experience, inventory, different types of weapons and armours and different types of enemies such as golems, slimes, and ogres. There will be a story added soon or later when I get time to do it. This is my first major C# program I made in my first year of Game Programming.
